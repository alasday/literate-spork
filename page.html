Here's what you're gonna need to do to use js in your site! It's really not that bad!

Change <html> in your html doc to <class="no-js" lang="en" dir="ltr">

add this:
<meta charset="utf-8">
<meta http-equiv="x-ua-compatible" content="ie=edge">
<meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
to the beginning of your html header
